    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is a Datathon?

    A Datathon is a data-centric competition where participants are tasked with analyzing datasets, building predictive models, and deriving insights to solve real-world problems within a limited time frame.

    Who can participate in the Datathon?

    Anyone with an interest in data science, machine learning, and analytics can participate. We welcome students, professionals, and data enthusiasts at all skill levels. You can participate individually or in teams of up to 4 members.

    How will the data be provided?

    The datasets for the competition will be released at the start of the Datathon. Participants will have access to download the data files from the competition portal.

    What tools or software can I use?

    Participants are free to use any tools, programming languages (e.g., Python, R), and libraries (e.g., TensorFlow, scikit-learn) for data analysis and model building.

    How will the submissions be evaluated?

    Submissions will be evaluated based on criteria such as model accuracy, innovative approach, insights drawn, and the clarity of the solution. The judging panel will include data experts and industry professionals.

    How do I register my team for the Datathon?

    You can register your team via the official SynergyX Datathon portal. Simply fill out the team details, pay the registration fee, and submit. You will receive a confirmation email once the registration is complete.

    What happens if I need help during the Datathon?

    We will have support staff available throughout the competition to assist with any technical issues or questions related to the dataset. You can reach out to them via the designated help channel provided after registration.
