    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the "CEO Talk - Insights and Innovations" event about?

    The "CEO Talk - Insights and Innovations" is an exclusive event where leaders from top tech companies discuss emerging trends in data, AI, and innovation. It is a must-attend for professionals looking to stay ahead of industry changes.

    When and where is the event taking place?

    The event will be held on 8-9 November 2024 at the Dr. Satyendra Nath Bose Academic Building, Khulna University.

    How long does the event last, and are meals provided?

    The event lasts for 6 days, from 23-28 May, and includes free lunch and coffee breaks each day.

    Who is the keynote speaker for the event?

    The keynote speaker is Laurence Francis, CEO of Google, who will be speaking multiple times throughout the event.

    How can I register for the event?

    You can register by clicking the "Registration" button located in the top-right corner of the event page.
