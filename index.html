Frequently Asked Questions

What is SynergyX 2024?

SynergyX 2024 is a two-day event combining a symposium and a datathon, focused on technology, innovation, and data. The symposium features keynote speakers from industry leaders, while the datathon challenges participants to solve real-world problems using big data and machine learning.

Who can participate in the Datathon?

The datathon is open to students, professionals, and data enthusiasts from all fields. Teams of up to 3 participants are allowed, and prior experience with big data and machine learning is recommended but not mandatory.

How can I register for the Symposium and Datathon?

To register for either the symposium or the datathon, visit the SynergyX 2024 website and click the "Register Now" button. You'll be guided through a simple registration form where you can choose to attend either or both events.

What is the deadline for registration?

The registration deadline for both the symposium and the datathon is October 15, 2024. Be sure to register before this date to secure your spot at SynergyX 2024!

Is there a registration fee?

There is no fee for attending the symposium, but there is a small entry fee for participating in the datathon to help cover organizational costs. Pricing details are available on the registration page.

Where will SynergyX 2024 be held?

SynergyX 2024 will take place at the University Campus. Specific venue details and directions will be provided upon registration and can also be found on the event website.

Do I need to bring anything for the Datathon?

Yes, participants are expected to bring their own laptops and any necessary equipment. All datasets and problem statements will be provided on the day of the event.

What are the prizes for the Datathon?

The top-performing teams in the datathon will be awarded cash prizes. The first-place team will receive $500, and the second-place team will win $300. All participants will also receive certificates of participation.

How can I stay updated on event details?

Once you register, you'll receive email updates with all the necessary information about the event. You can also follow us on our social media channels for the latest updates, schedules, and more.
